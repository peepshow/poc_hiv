Chapter 8: CBO and IDU

In this chapter we are exploring two different areas. The work of Community Based Organisations or CBO, and some specific aspects of HIV for intravenous drug users or IDU. CBO are groups formed by and for specific HIV+ communities. Often established by people who were once of the same risk groups but who now offer support for people newly diagnosed or struggling to manage their health. A kind of peer support network. These CBO provide many services and a kind of support that is more empathetic in nature that is not typically possible with government agencies. IDU and the use of Methadone in care are the other part of this chapter. This section is really important due to the heavy burden of HIV that IDU carried historically in Vietnam.
