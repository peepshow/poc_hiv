Chapter 8: CBO and IDU

In this chapter we are exploring two different areas. The work of Community Based Organisations or CBO, and some specific aspects of HIV for intravenous drug users or IDU.CBO are groups formed by and for specific HIV+ communities. Often established by people of the same risk groups, but who now offer support for people newly diagnosed or struggling to manage their health. CBO provide an important peer support network. The use of Methadone in controlling drug addiction plays a significant part in controlling HIV/AIDS in Vietnam. Learn how this important treatment is managed in Ho Chi Minh City and how it helps communities at risk..
