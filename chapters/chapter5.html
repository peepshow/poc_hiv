Chapter 5: ARV

ARV are the life preserving drugs that now allow PLWHIV the possibility of living a normal life expectancy. However with great treatment comes great risk and the inconsistent use of ARV can result in drug resistance. As are only 3 or 3 regimen for ARV this presents quite a challenge for PLWHIV. Taking the right pills everyday for their remaining life, at the right time of day. Not easy for the most organsied person let alone the often poorer HIV community members.
