Chapter 4: Testing and Diagnosis

HIV testing and diagnosis is the most essential aspect of both care and prevention. Early testing means people are aware of their HIV status and can take steps to prevent onwards transmission to others. They can also begin to access the support and care they need to manage their illness. Learn more about this essential aspect of care and prevention.
