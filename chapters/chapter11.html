Chapter 11: Policy and The Future

The future of HIV control in Vietnam does not lie on a clear path. With a number of major changes to how treatment and testing is funded and delivered, there are a lot of open questions that will need to be answered very soon indeed. International funds are all withdrawing, bringing a raft of changes that fundamentally change how care has been given. A new priority placed on efficient services that are light and effective, which are to be coupled with a drive to share costs between the government and the HIV+ community through insurance. While all sides try to remain positive there is a lot to be worked out and many of the often-poor PLWHIV are more than anxious about the future.
