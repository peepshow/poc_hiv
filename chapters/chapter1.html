Chapter 1: What it is

Explore this chapter to learn more about the science of HIV. Even if you know a lot, there will be more to hear and think about in this chapter. The virus is highly adapted to survive in humans and even to avoid defeat by any obvious means. So far the only way to beat HIV/AIDS is to either not get infected or to control the virus for life with drugs. Only recently have the ARV drugs become good enough to offer a full life expectancy but due to the recent HIV emergence, no one has been able to test that theory in full. There will be many innovations in the coming decades around HIV but where do our hopes for elimination lie?
