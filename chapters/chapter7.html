Chapter 7: Outpatient and Inpatient

Outpatient care begins with testing in the community and can supply basic care to PLWHIV. Once there are complications caused by HIV that cannot be treated as an outpatient then they may seek care in an inpatient hospital ward. This chapter explores the specific roles, tasks and challenges that each kind of care faces. Both levels of care are likely to be seen by someone living with HIV at some point, so these services are critical in controlling HIV in the community.
